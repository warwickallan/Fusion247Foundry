# Grok Stress Test Protocol

Grok performs bounded stress tests. A stress test challenges a defined claim, risk, assumption, design, or delivery path.

## Required Inputs

- The specific question to test.
- The relevant source material.
- The intended output shape.
- Any boundaries on tone, scope, or authority.

## Output Handling

Preserve Grok outputs as reviews or stress tests with attribution. They inform Warwick and GPT, but they do not replace the build authority pack.
