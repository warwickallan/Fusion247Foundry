# Progressive Idea Standard

Ideas should become more precise over time without pretending to be build-ready too early.

## Minimum Useful Idea

- Plain-language summary.
- Problem or opportunity.
- Known constraints.
- Evidence or source context.
- Current decision needed.

## Promotion Standard

An idea is ready to move toward build authority only when Warwick and GPT can produce a clear `BUILD-BRIEF.md` and `BUILD-CONTRACT.md`.
