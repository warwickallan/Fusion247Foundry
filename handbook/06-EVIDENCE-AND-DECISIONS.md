# Evidence And Decisions

## Evidence

Evidence includes source pages, user statements, technical observations, stress tests, screenshots, logs, and prior decisions.

## Decisions

Record decisions with:

- decision;
- owner;
- date;
- context;
- consequence;
- linked evidence.

## Migration Note

Fable-authored informal pages are authored reviews or stress tests, not disposable material. Preserve them with attribution and original ClickUp title provenance.
