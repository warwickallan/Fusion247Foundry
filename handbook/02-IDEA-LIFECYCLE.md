# Idea Lifecycle

## States

- Captured: idea exists but has not been shaped.
- Exploring: Warwick and GPT are clarifying the opportunity and constraints.
- Stress Testing: Grok is performing bounded challenge or review.
- Briefed: `BUILD-BRIEF.md` exists.
- Contracted: `BUILD-CONTRACT.md` exists and is the build authority.
- Planning: Larry is preparing `IMPLEMENTATION-PLAN.md`.
- Ready: the three-document pack is complete and approved.
- Archived: idea is retained for provenance but is not active.

Lifecycle values imported from ClickUp remain provisional until reconciled against the Current State pages and Idea Register cards.
